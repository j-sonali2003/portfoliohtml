The Best Movies According to Sonali

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

Heartwarming comedy-drama film that explores friendship, ambition, and the flaws of the Indian education system.

Tadap

Romantic thriller that captivates audiences with its intense storyline and powerful performances.

Insidious

Chilling horror film renowned for its spine-tingling atmosphere, clever scares, and unsettling storyline.
